PIZZA Recipe

Standard Margherita Pizza

This will be a recipe for how to make Margherita Pizza. It will contain a list of ingredients and steps to follow.

Ingredients :

  • flour
  • vodka sauce
  • mozzerella cheese
  • basil leaf
  • water

Instructions:

  1. Mix water with dough. Roll into a ball & rest
  2. Roll out dough into a flat circle
  3. layaer sauce on top of dough
  4. add vodka sauce, mozzerella and basil leaves
  5. Bake to desired doneness. Rest and enjoy!
